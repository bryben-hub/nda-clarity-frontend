What is an NDA Review?

An NDA review is a professional analysis of a non-disclosure agreement to identify:

  • Unfair confidentiality terms that limit your future opportunities
  • Excessive non-compete clauses that could trap you
  • Unclear intellectual property ownership
  • Unreasonable duration periods
  • One-sided liability clauses
  • Hidden termination gotchas

Why You Need an NDA Reviewed

Most people sign NDAs without proper review. This can lead to:

  • Lost job opportunities - overly broad non-competes prevent you from working in your field
  • Legal liability - undefined terms leave you exposed to lawsuits
  • Stolen intellectual property - unclear IP clauses mean you don't own your own work
  • Endless obligations - NDAs with no end date bind you forever
